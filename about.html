Our Story

How it all began

The Minecraft Chronicles was founded in 2025 by a passionate Minecraft player who wanted to create the definitive resource for Minecraft's history and mechanics.

What started as a small fan project has grown into a comprehensive archive documenting every aspect of Minecraft's development, from its earliest versions to the modern game we know today.

This consists of an individual historian, and a technical player who shares a deep love for Minecraft and its incredible journey.
