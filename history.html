Minecraft Through the Ages

Explore the evolution of the world's most popular sandbox game

Development Timeline

From Cave Game to Global Phenomenon
2009

The Birth of Minecraft

Markus "Notch" Persson begins development on "Cave Game," releasing the first public version (Minecraft Classic) on May 17.

Classic 0.0.11a
2010

Alpha & Beta Era

Introduction of survival mode, biomes, beds, and the Nether dimension. The game gains rapid popularity.

Beta 1.8
2011

Official Release

Minecraft 1.0 launches at MineCon, adding The End dimension and Ender Dragon boss fight.

Release 1.0
2014

Microsoft Acquisition

Microsoft acquires Mojang for $2.5 billion. Notch leaves the company but development continues.

Version 1.8
2016

Combat Update

Major combat overhaul introduces shields, dual-wielding, and new enchantments.

Version 1.9
2020

Nether Update

Complete Nether overhaul with new biomes, mobs, and Netherite gear.

Version 1.16
