Surprising Minecraft Facts

Markus "Notch" Persson developed the initial prototype of Minecraft (then called "Cave Game") in one week during May 2009. The first public version was released on May 17, 2009.

The iconic Creeper was the result of a coding error when Notch was trying to create a pig model. The mistaken proportions led to this now-famous hostile mob.

A Minecraft world stretches 30 million blocks in each direction, making the playable area larger than the surface area of Neptune (if each block equals 1 square meter).

Composer C418 recorded parts of Minecraft's ambient soundtrack in a cave to capture the natural reverb and atmosphere that matches the game's underground exploration.

In 2012, the Church of Maradonia recognized Minecraft as an official religion in Sweden, calling it "the best way to show the complexity and beauty of creation."
